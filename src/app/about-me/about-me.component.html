VISION

Inspired by nature, designed for the future.

Since its creation in 2018, Ubac has offered sneakers in natural and recycled materials.

The word Ubac designates the side of the mountain least exposed to the sun and therefore the most preserved of the human footprint. Ubac then becomes a symbol of a preserved and still wild nature which must be taken care of. It aspires to a feeling of freedom and protection that high mountain landscapes can provide.
